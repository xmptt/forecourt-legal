Privacy Policy

Forecourt — Last updated 1 May 2026

Forecourt is operated by Raymond John Wellard. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and how it is handled.

What data is collected

Account information

When you sign in with Apple, I receive your name and an anonymous Apple ID. These are stored on the server to identify your account and personalise the app.

Vehicle details

Vehicles you add to the app — including make, model, year, fuel type, tank size, MPG, driving style, registration number, MOT expiry date, tax due date, colour, engine size, and CO2 emissions — are stored on the server linked to your account to power savings calculations, MOT reminders, and the garage screen.

Fuel fill-up records

When you log a fill-up, the details (litres, price per litre, total cost, station, date, odometer reading, and calculated MPG) are stored on the server linked to your account to power fill-up history, monthly reports, and real-world MPG tracking.

Location

Your precise location is sent to the server when you search for nearby stations. It is used only to run the search query and is not stored or associated with your account.

The station you fill up at is saved as part of your fill-up record, which indirectly links a location to your account.

Push notification token

If you allow notifications, your device token is sent to the server so that price alerts and monthly fuel reports can be delivered to your device. It is not used for advertising or tracking.

What data is not collected

  • I do not collect your email address
  • I do not track you across other apps or websites
  • I do not serve advertising
  • I do not sell or share your data with third parties

How data is stored

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway (railway.app) in the European Union. Data is transmitted over HTTPS at all times.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact me at the address below and I will process your request within 30 days.
